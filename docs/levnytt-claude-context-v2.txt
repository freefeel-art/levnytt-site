# LevNytt Article Production Rules

## Language

Swedish only.

---

## Audience

Swedish readers interested in:

- health
- nutrition
- supplements
- NeoLife science
- healthy aging

---

## Article Length

Minimum:
2200 words

Target:
2600-2800 words

Maximum:
3000 words

---

## Content Hierarchy

Always use sources in this order:

1. LevNytt Facts Library
2. NeoLife Scientific Material
3. Peer-reviewed research
4. External sources

Never skip Level 1.

---

## Fact Policy

Never invent:

- studies
- participant counts
- percentages
- statistics
- NeoLife product claims

If uncertain:

Use qualitative wording.

Prefer:

- research suggests
- may support
- has been associated with

Avoid:

- proven
- guaranteed
- best

---

## Images

Every article contains:

1 Hero Image (AI)

1 Infographic (AI)

1 Official NeoLife Product Image

Hero image is never a product image.

---

## Structure

Required:

- H1
- Quick Answer
- Key Takeaways
- Main Content
- FAQ
- Sources

---

## Internal Linking

Every article should link to:

- NeoLife Vetenskap
- NeoLife Historia

Plus:

1-3 relevant product pages

1-3 relevant informational pages

---

## Product Mentions

Answer the question first.

Products come later.

Never write sales copy.

Single publish-ready HTML file.

# LevNytt Principles

- Fakta före hype
- Värde före pris
- Konsekvens före perfektion
- Långsiktighet före genvägar
- Förstå först, besluta sedan
- Nyfikenhet före tvärsäkerhet

These principles guide:
- article writing
- CTA design
- internal linking
- authority pages
- pull quotes
- product positioning

Pull quotes should reinforce a LevNytt Principle when appropriate, but are never required.

# Pull Quote Rules

Purpose:

Pull quotes highlight important ideas,
not famous people.

Rules:

- Optional, never required.
- Use 0-2 pull quotes per article.
- Pull quotes should reinforce a LevNytt Principle.
- Prefer original article insights over external quotes.
- Warren Buffett, Steve Jobs or other sources may inspire the idea,
  but the pull quote should normally be written in LevNytt's own voice.
- Pull quotes must be short, memorable and easy to scan.
- Do not force pull quotes into articles that do not naturally contain one.

Examples:

"Fakta före hype."

"Värde handlar om vad du får, inte bara vad du betalar."

"Konsekvens slår nästan alltid perfektion."

"Långsiktighet är ofta underskattad eftersom resultaten syns sent."
