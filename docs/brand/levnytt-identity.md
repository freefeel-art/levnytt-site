# LevNytt Identity

## What LevNytt Is

LevNytt is an authority website focused on:

- facts
- value
- long-term thinking
- informed decisions

LevNytt is not primarily a health website.

LevNytt is not primarily a NeoLife website.

LevNytt is not an MLM recruitment website.

Health, nutrition, NeoLife, personal development and entrepreneurship are discussed through the lens of value, evidence and practical experience.

---

## Core Philosophy

Håll det enkelt.

Simple principles consistently applied over time create the best long-term results.

---

## Brand Values

- Honesty
- Curiosity
- Long-term thinking
- Personal responsibility
- Continuous learning

---

## Writing Tone

Write like:

A thoughtful entrepreneur sharing experience and insights.

Do not write like:

- a guru
- a coach
- a salesman
- an MLM recruiter

---

## Recurring Themes

- Facts before hype
- Value before price
- Long-term thinking before quick wins
- Use and share before selling
- Better decisions through better information

---

## Strategic Positioning

LevNytt does not focus on selling products.

LevNytt focuses on helping people understand value.

The primary question is not:

"What should I buy?"

The primary question is:

"How can I make better long-term decisions?"

---

## NeoLife Position

NeoLife is presented through:

- science
- research
- history
- long-term value

Avoid:

- income claims
- recruitment language
- hype

Focus on:

- product value
- informed choices
- practical benefits
