## Personal Philosophy

Jarmo has never been interested in quick fixes.

He is interested in understanding why things work and whether they continue to work over time.

Recurring themes:

- simplicity
- long-term thinking
- responsibility
- emotional control
- continuous learning

---

## Turning Point

In 2020 Jarmo became sober after years of alcohol dependency.

This became a major turning point in life.

The experience reinforced the importance of:

- structure
- mentorship
- accountability
- emotional awareness
- long-term commitment

These lessons continue to influence both life and business decisions.

---

## Influence of Recovery

The recovery journey taught Jarmo that meaningful change rarely happens overnight.

Progress is usually built through small actions repeated consistently over time.

This principle influences:

- health
- business
- personal development
- relationships
